About me

Xiaoou Wang, founder of the website AIPrototypes, follow me on Google Scholar and Medium :)

NLP researcher particularly interested in:

1. Computational argumentation: namely Argument Mining and Argument Generation, including also Counter-argument Generation and their applications to the fight against online dis/misinformation
2. Advanced error type classification via Grammatical Error Correction (GEC, especially transformer-based Neural machine translation (NMT) approaches)
3. Advanced evaluation of students' written production
  • [nlp/ml] Automated Essay Scoring (AES based on error analysis)
  • [nlp/ml/dl] Similarity-based evaluation with Gold Standard
  • [dl] Identification of argumentative elements without Gold Standard
4. Creating benchmark datasets in tasks related to commonsense reasoning and developing robust evaluation protocols
5. In a broad sense, promoting/democratizing trustworthy, interpretable and socially beneficial Artificiel Intelligence
  • Semi-automatic analysis of biomedical texts, popular science article written in French for DemoTal (project supported by the French Ministry of Culture).
  • Better understand spell checkers to make informed decisions, idem.
  • Argue instead of blocking: moderating online comments while promoting debate, idem.
  • Beyond the simple positive-negative dichotomy, discover the aspect-based sentiment analysis (ABSA), idem.
  • Extractive question answering from customer reviews, idem.
  • Conducting POCs with limited data? From data quality to zero-shot learning, idem.
